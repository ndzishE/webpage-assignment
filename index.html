Taco Trivia

When did Tacos first appear in South Africa?

The first Mexican fast-food restaurant in South Africa, Senor Pico, opened in Johannesburg introducing tacos and other Mexican dishes to the country, according to The Christian Science Monitor. The owner, a South African who developed a taste for Mexican food while living in California, opened the restaurant to cater to the growing interest in diverse cuisine from 1982. (source: The Christian Science Monitor)
